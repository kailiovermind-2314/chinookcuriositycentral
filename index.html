Welcome to the Beginning.

We are a student-led, non-profit initiative committed to contributing to the Calgary community by empowering youth through supporting and fostering their curiosity. Founded in 2023, this organization has been promoting the gestation of curiosity in the minds of youth all around Calgary.

Chinook Curiosity Central organizes open contests with creative operational styles throughout the school year. The goal is to inspire contestants to explore what they have never explored, and guide their curiosities in new directions.

Executive's Message

Curious. Aspiring. Motivators. Every year, the Chinook Curiosity Central team strives to inspire curiosity into the local community. Unlike energy or momentum, curiosity is not conserved, and it has a generative ability that truly puts the aforementioned conservation laws into question. We are not extreme-achieving students nor are we geniuses (but we hope you are). We are simply curious, and we want to imbue that quality into you.

In the face of academia and all the distractions of society, students may often discover that the youthful zeal they possessed has been lost. Not lost, but simply buried. To understand the unknown is a fundamental desire, not something that should fade with age. Our mission is to make you curious like your youthful self, hence the title "Welcome to the Beginning."

We are not superiors nor mentors. We are learners. Welcome to Chinook Curiosity Central.

Our Organization

Chinook Curiosity Central is a student-led, non-profit initiative committed to contributing to the Calgary community by empowering youth through support and fostering their curiosity. Founded by Sir Winston Churchill High School IBDP Student Weidong Zhang in 2023, Chinook Curiosity Central is a product of reflection. Students are trained to be academically inclined from a very young age, restricting their pursuit of personal interests.

Chinook Curiosity Central organized its first contest last year at Calgary Central Library with astounding success. This year, we aim to expand this mission even further by expanding the organization to new heights with non-traditional contest styles. These contests are interdisciplinary, with many opportunities to learn and apply. These are contests of curiosity.

Our mission is to transform the conventional academic mindset of Calgarian students through contests and seminars, to name a few. These missions are incentives to push the stagnant wheel of curiosity and keep the youth of Calgary curious and inquisitive. We know that curiosity is a state that engenders creation and progress - so we will keep on going.
