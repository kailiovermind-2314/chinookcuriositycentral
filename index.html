Our Organization

We are a student-led, non-profit initiative committed to contributing to the Calgary community by empowering youth through support and fostering their curiosity. Founded in 2023, this organization has been promoting the gestation of curiosity in the minds of hundreds.

The Enigma Express

Since 2023 Chinook Curiosity Central's founder, Weidong Zhang, has been running this Spotify podcast to inspire thought and imbibe curiosity in its readers. This year, we are taking this podcast to another level by exploring all sorts of questions ranging from pure mathematics to biology and even to literature.

Our goal is not to simply give you knowledge. There is always too much to discover. It is a matter of mindset - to realize that there will always be more in this world, and it is never too late to learn. Keep searching.
