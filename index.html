Welcome to the Beginning.

Chinook Curiosity Central is a student-led, non-profit initiative committed to empowering youth through curiosity. We operate in the Calgary area. If you are an ambitious youth in Calgary looking for a different type of learning experience, join our Discord server to be notified for all future events.

Why Curiosity?

cu·ri·os·i·ty

/ˌkyo͝orēˈäsədē/

noun

1. The desire to do something even with no clear benefit in mind.

2. The innate human drive to venture into the unknown.

At Chinook Curiosity Central, we believe that curiosity is never lost, but rather buried under the weight of academic pressures and distractions. Our mission is to help you recover this youthful zeal for learning, hence the title "Welcome to the Beginning."

Thank you to everybody that attended the 2024 Curiosity Contest. Our contest featured three rounds and a mix of both math and science questions. The event was successful in spite of a cyberattack. 2023 was when Chinook Curiosity Central got rolling. CCC acquired sponsorships from four different sources and received CTV coverage.
